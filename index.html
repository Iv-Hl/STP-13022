About the Game

Fast-paced cooking that'll test your multitasking skills

So basically, Cooking Tour throws you into these crazy busy kitchens where you're scrambling to cook meals and upgrade your equipment while everything gets more hectic. Each level throws new recipes at you, plus the customers get pickier, but watching everything come together on screen is really satisfying. It's one of those games that's super easy to learn but then you find yourself playing way longer than you planned - great for killing time but with enough unlocks to keep you hooked. We want it to feel energetic and fun - like you're actually in a bustling kitchen.
